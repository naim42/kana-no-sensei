Hi there!

I'm a French teenager who likes to study the japanese language on his free time.
I developed this tool to help me learn hiragana and katakana, the two japanese syllabaries.
You can train on either of them, with dakuten or not, and with the youon forms or not.
The source code for this project is available on GitHub.
